## Customer Support & Experience

This document describes the customer data and support system for an e-commerce platform.

### Customer Complaint Monitoring

Customer complaints are recorded and aggregated by product, category, and time period. Common complaint patterns are detectable across the customer base.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Did customer complaints increase yesterday?"
- "What are the most common complaints from the past 24 hours?"
- "Is there a common issue being reported by customers today?"
- "Which products are generating the most complaints this week?"
- "Are complaints concentrated in a specific region or customer tier?"
- "Correlate yesterday's complaint spike with the sales drop."

### Refund & Return Experience

Customer-facing refund requests and return experiences are tracked separately from the operational refund lifecycle. High return rates often correlate with negative reviews and complaint spikes.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Are returns or refund requests higher than usual this week?"
- "Did negative return experiences increase after yesterday's orders?"
- "Which customers submitted multiple refund requests recently?"

### Reviews & Ratings

Product reviews are linked to customer profiles. Negative review spikes can signal quality issues or fulfilment problems that affect conversions.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Are there negative reviews affecting conversions on top products?"
- "Did any product receive a flood of 1-star reviews yesterday?"
- "What are customers saying about the Laptop category this week?"
- "Show reviews from Gold and Platinum tier customers about Electronics."

### Customer Profiles & Segmentation

Customers have: ID, name, email, region, loyalty tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum), and lifetime spend. Segments can be queried by any combination.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Find customer john.doe@example.com."
- "Show all Platinum customers in North America."
- "Which customers have spent more than $5,000 lifetime?"
- "List all Gold-tier customers in Europe who purchased Electronics."

### Customer Order Analysis

Order frequency, average order value, payment method preference, and channel usage are tracked per customer.

Realistic operational questions:
- "What is the order pattern for customers who complained yesterday?"
- "Did high-value customers reduce their purchase frequency this week?"
- "Compare order behaviour between Bronze and Platinum tier customers."

### Newsletter & Campaigns — Human Approval Required

Bulk newsletters or promotional messages to customer segments require human approval before sending.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Send a recovery discount to all customers who had a failed order yesterday." — triggers HITL approval
- "Notify subscribed Platinum customers about restocked products." — triggers HITL approval
- "How many customers are subscribed to the newsletter in North America?"
