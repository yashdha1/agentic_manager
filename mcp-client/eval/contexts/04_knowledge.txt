## Marketing, Campaigns & Policy

This document describes the internal knowledge base covering marketing campaigns, strategies, and business policies.

### Campaign Performance Monitoring

Each campaign has: name, objective, start/end dates, channels (email, paid search, social, influencer), budget, ROI, and current status (active, paused, completed).

Realistic operational questions:
- "Were any campaigns paused or underperforming yesterday?"
- "Did campaign performance drop compared to last week?"
- "Did we miss any scheduled promotions yesterday?"
- "Which marketing channel performed the worst yesterday?"
- "What was the ROI of the Black Friday 2024 campaign?"
- "Are there any active campaigns running on out-of-stock products?"
- "Which campaigns are currently active and what are their daily spend levels?"

### Campaign Strategy Updates — Human Approval Required

Modifying campaign tactics, budget allocation, or channel mix requires human approval.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Pause the worst-performing campaign." — triggers HITL approval
- "Shift budget from the underperforming paid search campaign to social." — triggers HITL approval
- "Update the Summer Sale campaign to exclude out-of-stock products." — triggers HITL approval

### Discount & Promotional Strategy

The platform uses targeted discounts to recover sales during downturns. Discounts above 25% require manager-level approval.

Realistic operational questions:
- "Should we run a discount to recover yesterday's sales drop?"
- "Run a 10% discount on the top 3 underperforming products." — triggers HITL approval
- "Is a 30% discount on Smartphones compliant with our discount policy?"
- "Did discounts help recover sales in previous downturns?"
- "Which discount level has historically worked best for Electronics?"

### Business Policies

Key policies include:
- Refund policy: 30-day window for defective items; condition assessed before approval.
- Discount policy: promotions above 25% require manager approval; seasonal schedules apply.
- Shipping policy: free shipping on domestic orders above $50; international orders have a flat fee.
- Return policy: physical condition of returned items assessed before refund approval.

Policy statuses: active, archived, draft. Status changes require human approval.

Realistic operational questions:
- "What is our current refund policy?"
- "Does the discount policy allow 30% off on Electronics?"
- "What are the shipping rules for international orders?"
- "Archive the old free-returns policy." — triggers HITL approval
- "Activate the updated discount policy draft." — triggers HITL approval

Realistic operational questions:
